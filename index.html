ABOUT
Noah Grayson, B.S., PORP, MCRTA
Offensive Security Engineer
I am an information security engineer with an offensive focus, experienced in systematically evaluating the security of complex systems and environments.
My security assessment experience includes ethical hacking, penetration testing, and red team operations support. I have practical penetration testing and ethical hacking experience with corporate and wireless networks, multi-cloud infrastructure, web and LLM applications, and aerospace platforms. I have practical red team operations support experience in open-source intelligence (OSINT) investigation and reporting, operations security (OPSEC), and cyber threat intelligence (CTI).
My security engineering experience includes the design, development, administration, and operation of security tools and methodologies across various environments. I have experience in securing technologies and solutions for networks (firewalls and software-defined networking), cloud infrastructure (Azure and AWS), and applications (Docker and Kubernetes).
My security research experience includes investigating advanced global threats (counterintelligence, counterterrorism, and organized crime studies), emerging and disruptive technologies (AI, quantum, nano, bio, and neuro technologies), and cognitive warfare (information, psychological, and cyber domains).
My future professional development is focused on red team operations (advanced exploitation and post-exploitation, stealth and evasion, and adversarial simulation), application security engineering (full-stack development, cloud security, and DevSecOps), and information security research (reverse engineering, malware analysis, and software exploitation).
